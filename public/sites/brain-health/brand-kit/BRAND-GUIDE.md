# Brain Health and Chiropractic - Brand Guide

## Typography

### Primary Font: Playfair Display
- **Usage:** Headlines, hero text, section titles
- **Weights:** 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-Bold), 700 (Bold)
- **Source:** Google Fonts
- **URL:** https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Playfair+Display

### Secondary Font: Open Sans
- **Usage:** Body text, navigation, buttons, captions
- **Weights:** 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-Bold), 700 (Bold)
- **Source:** Google Fonts
- **URL:** https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Open+Sans

## Logo Usage

- **Primary Logo:** `newlogo.jpg` - Full logo with brain icon and text
- **Minimum Size:** 120px width for digital
- **Clear Space:** Maintain padding equal to the height of the "B" in BRAIN around all sides
- **Background:** Use on white or light backgrounds; for dark backgrounds, use white version

---

## Button Styles

### Primary Button (CTA)
- Background: Linear gradient `#60a5fa` to `#3b82f6`
- Text: White (`#ffffff`)
- Border Radius: 50px (pill shape)
- Shadow: `0 4px 15px rgba(59, 130, 246, 0.3)`

### Secondary/Outline Button
- Background: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)` with backdrop blur
- Border: 2px solid `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.8)`
- Text: White on dark backgrounds
