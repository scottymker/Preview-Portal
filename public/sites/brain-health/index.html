Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Cooper Dykstra DC, CFMP

Functional Neurologist | Chiropractor | Your Partner in Healing

I understand what you're going through because I've been there.

In 2008, I was ejected from a vehicle in a serious accident. I couldn't move my left leg for six weeks and battled post-concussion symptoms including headaches, depression, anxiety, brain fog, and fatigue for nearly 10 years.

Traditional medicine couldn't help me. It wasn't until I discovered the combination of chiropractic care, functional neurology, and functional medicine that I finally found relief.

That experience drives my passion today. I founded Brain Health and Chiropractic so I can help as many people as possible find relief and fast-track their healing journey.

Certifications & Training:

  • Certified Functional Medicine Practitioner (CFMP)
  • Functional Neurology with Dr. Kharrazian & Dr. Brock
  • Concussion/mTBI Program - Carrick Institute
  • Doctorate of Chiropractic - NWHSU
